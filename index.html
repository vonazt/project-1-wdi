GAME OF SHINING THRONES

HOW TO PLAY

BASICS CONTROLS CHARACTER TYPES

Game of Shining Thrones is a strategy game for two players.

Each PLAYER controls an army of six CHARACTERS, taking it in turns to move around the BATTLEFIELD and ATTACK or cast MAGIC on the other player's CHARACTERS.

Each CHARACTER has a certain amount of HEALTH, represented by HIT POIINTS (HP).
When a CHARACTER's HP reaches 0, they die.

The winnner is the PLAYER who wipes out all the CHARACTERS in the opposing army.

CONTROLS

MOVE CHARACTER: Use the arrow keys to move your character around the screen. The available squares are highlighted in white and are dictated by the CHARACTER's AGILITY.

SWITCH CHARACTER: Press TAB at the beginning of your turn to select your CHARACTER. You must do this BEFORE moving any CHARACTER.

SELECT DEFENDER: If your CHARACTER is within ATTACK or MAGIC range of more than one DEFENDER, press S to switch between DEFENDERS.

ATTACK or MAGIC: Click on either ATTACK or MAGIC when they turn white in the OPTIONS window to use them.

DEFEND: Click DEFEND in the OPTIONS window to end your turn. This has no other effect.

CHARACTER TYPES

There are two types of character: MELEE and MAGIC.

MELEE: Melee characters can deal a lot of DAMAGE (DMG) to an enemy's HP with their ATTACK, but have low AGILITY.

To make an ATTACK a melee character must be in an adjacent square to a DEFENDER. If an ATTACK is possible, the ATTACK option will turn WHITE in the OPTIONS window.

The amount of DAMAGE (DMG) done is determined by the ATTACKER's DMG stat and also the DEFENDER's DEFENCE (DEF) stat.

After making an ATTACK, the PLAYER's turn will end.

MAGIC: Magic characters can cast MAGIC from range and can move long distances thanks to high AGILITY, but are vulnerable to ATTACK.

A MAGIC character can cast a spell from two squares away from a DEFENDER. The MAGIC option will turn WHITE in the OPTIONS window if this is available.

The amount of damage done by the spell to the DEFENDER's HP is determined by the character's MAGIC DAMAGE (M. DMG) stat and the DEFENDER's DEFENCE (DEF).

There are two types of magic: ICE and FIRE.

ICE magic will decrease the DEFENDER's DMG stat, along with their HP.

FIRE magic will decrease the DEFENDER's DEF stat, along with their HP.

Each MAGIC CHARACTER has a certain amount of MAGIC POINTS (MP).

Every spell cast has a certain COST that will deplete the character's MP by the same amount.

When MP reaches 0, the MAGIC CHARACTER can no longer cast spells.
